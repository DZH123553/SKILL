## 1. 基本事实与履历

- **中文名**：冯波
- **身份标签**：中国第一代 VC（first-generation Chinese venture capitalist）、Dragonfly Capital 创始 Managing Partner、Ceyuan Ventures 联合创始人

- **职业轨迹**：
  - **1994**：加入 **Robertson Stephens**（美国高科技投资银行），开创其中国业务
  - **1994-1997**：Robertson Stephens 中国业务期间最著名 deal——发现并带 **Sina Corp（新浪公司，Weibo 母体）上市**
  - **1997.12**：加入 **China Venture Capital Co., Ltd.** 作为 **Chief Representative**（RootData 记录）
  - **1999**：**联合创立 Ceyuan Ventures（启源创投）**，founding partner
  - **2000-2013**：Ceyuan 投资一批中国互联网 / 消费 / 企业服务公司
  - **2014**：**第一笔 crypto 投资——OKEx**（北京起家的加密交易所）
  - **2018.10**：**与 Alexander Pack 共同创立 Dragonfly Capital**（原名 Dragonfly Capital Partners），首期 $100M
  - **~2020**：Alexander Pack 离开 Dragonfly；Haseeb Qureshi 加入为 Managing Partner
  - **2021.03**：Dragonfly Fund II 公布，**$225M**
  - **2022.04**：Dragonfly Fund III **$650M**
  - **2025**：**Dragonfly 新 $650M 基金**募集（第四期）

## 2. Dragonfly 关键事实

- **创立**：2018.10
- **原始 co-founders**：Alexander Pack（前 Bain Capital Ventures，也曾在 AngelList 做 crypto micro-fund，投过 Polychain / Numerai）+ Bo Feng
- **现任 Managing Partners**：Bo Feng + Haseeb Qureshi
- **Fund 轨迹**：
  - Fund I（2018.10）：$100M，a16z 创始人 Marc Andreessen、Chris Dixon、Founders Fund 的 Cyan Banister、Polychain 的 Olaf Carlson-Wee、Sequoia China 沈南鹏、Zhenfund 徐小平 等参与；OKEx + Bitmain 作为 strategic investors
  - Fund II（2021.03）：$225M，Sequoia China、OKEx、Huobi、Bitmain、Bybit 加入 LP
  - Fund III（2022.04）：$650M
  - Fund IV（2025）：$650M，Haseeb 公开称 "biggest bet yet"
- **投资领域**：DeFi、NFT、Ethereum scalability (L2)、centralized financial infrastructure、crypto infrastructure、AI × crypto
- **LP 结构**：
  - 传统机构 LP：主权基金、捐赠、养老金
  - 亚洲 internet OG：**Baidu**、**Meituan-Dianping**、**Meitu**、**China Renaissance Bank** 创始人
  - VC LP：Sequoia China（沈南鹏）、Zhenfund（徐小平）、Midas List VCs
  - Silicon Valley：Marc Andreessen、Chris Dixon、Founders Fund Cyan Banister、Polychain Olaf Carlson-Wee、Bain Capital Ventures Salil Deshpande
  - Crypto 战略投资者：OKEx、Bitmain、Huobi、Bybit

## 3. Ceyuan Ventures 关键事实

- **创立**：1999
- **创始合伙人**：Bo Feng + 其他中国第一代 VC
- **定位**：中国最早的本土 VC 之一
- **投资领域**：中国互联网、企业服务、消费、tech
- **Portfolio（部分公开）**：中国 2000-2010 年代一批互联网公司

## 4. 关键引用

### 关于投资哲学（综合 TradersUnion）

> "Bo Feng's business philosophy revolves around leveraging the potential of emerging technologies to disrupt existing markets. He draws parallels between the 1990s internet boom and the current cryptocurrency market, seeing both as transformative, global opportunities."

> "His approach focuses on ecosystem-building, connecting key players and bridging gaps between geographies to foster innovation."

### 关于 Dragonfly 的 East-West bridge（Decrypt）

> "The bridge isn't so much about bringing much-needed global capital to Chinese entrepreneurs as it is about bringing Chinese market insight to global projects."

> "One example that comes to mind is dYdX, a crypto derivative DEX. Since trading crypto derivatives are banned for Americans, dYdX cannot market its derivative products to Americans. So what can they do? Go East."

### 关于进入 crypto 的契机（Decrypt）

> "Feng's first crypto deal was in 2014 when he invested in OKEx, a crypto exchange that started in Beijing. Through the eyes of OKEx, Feng saw the rise of the crypto economy replacing the traditional Internet economy."

### 关于 Dragonfly 创立动机（Forbes 2018, Alex Pack 发言，代表 Dragonfly 立场）

> "Crypto is a new asset class, and it made sense to have a new firm to support it. We thought older ones would have a disadvantage."

### 关于 Dragonfly 的生态组织力（Decrypt 描述 2019 summit）

> "Feng's ability to convene China's most important tech entrepreneurs and venture capitalists was seen during Dragonfly Capital's 2019 summit. There, he gathered crypto celebrities such as Leon Li from Huobi, Star Xu from OKEx, Jihan Wu from Bitmain, Nanpeng Shen from Sequoia China, Xing Wang from Meituan, Fred Ehrsam from Paradigm, and of course Vitalik, the God of them all."

### 关于 guanxi（Decrypt）

> "If there's one word business folks need to know in China, it's 'guanxi' which means 'connections'... China's crypto circle has its own web of connections."

> "Connections matter in real life, and they matter even more in crypto."

### 关于 Asia 与 decentralization（Decrypt 观察）

> "The Chinese crypto community cares much less about decentralization. Instead, they opt for usability. Dragonfly's understanding of the Chinese community could help them identify the best CeFi projects in China, or bring those CeFi projects from the west to Chinese audiences."

## 8. 个人特质与风格标签

- **第一代中国 VC**：少数同时深度参与中国互联网和 crypto 两个 wave 的投资人
- **Ecosystem-builder**：通过高质量 summit / LP network 聚合顶级 founder
- **Guanxi-driven**：关系网络极致丰富，从 90s 到 2020s 跨代持续
