## 4. 关键引用

### 关于 belief equals value（招牌哲学）

> "When talking to skeptics, what I talk about is this idea of value being equal to belief. The institutional establishment over the past hundred years has basically said what makes something valuable: a stock has cash flows, or a house has the ability for you to live in it and use it. But I think what's happened in the past year or so, with meme stocks, meme cryptocurrencies, and NFTs, is that random people on the internet don't really care what the institutional establishment says is valuable. If the collective group of people on the internet believes something has value, it has value." —— Decrypt 2021

> "We are in a world where belief equals value." —— Decrypt 2021

### 关于 market as truth

> "The great thing about markets is that, in the long run, the market kind of dictates the truth. People can be loud on social media and stuff, but ultimately the market is my favorite source of truth." —— Decrypt

### 关于 2013 入圈故事

> "My background, I kind of fell down the rabbit hole back in 2013. I was just a random person on the internet in Portland, Maine. A lot of people talk about why they fell down the rabbit hole... For me, what initially drew me was kind of the social movement that I saw happening online back then." —— Unchained

> "I read this Wired article about this magic internet money that was being used to buy guns and drugs on Silk Road. That was the early media narrative... I jumped onto Bitcoin Talk, which was the first online forum that Satoshi actually created and /r/bitcoin. And what I saw kind of underneath the surface of the media headlines, there was this really passionate community of people from around the world who weren't aligned by physical location... but they were aligned by a shared belief system... and economic incentives." —— Unchained

> "I've always been an online community person. So dating back to like middle school when I was interested in comic books, high school when I was interested in sneakers, I've always wanted to dive online to online forums." —— Unchained

### 关于 1confirmation 投资 thesis

> "Our thesis is very simple: back authentic founders that have deep historical context on crypto, that are building products that we understand as users." —— Unchained

> "I think there's a ton of different ways to make money in crypto. For us, we've kind of stuck to what we understand and what we get excited about as users. That has treated us well. And that's kind of what we're going to continue to do." —— Unchained

> "I think there's a lot of investors in the space that are trading based on memes or fundamentals — there's a whole different bunch of ways to make money. For us, it's products that we get excited about as users. By focusing on product, it allows you to kind of ignore a lot of the noise in the space." —— Unchained

### 关于 Bitcoin maximalism 的 evolution

> "I used to be a Bitcoin maximalist. At Coinbase, in 2013, we were Bitcoin maximalists. And over time, when we saw real innovations that were pushing the whole industry forward and bringing new people into cryptocurrency, that's what we ultimately cared about, and so we adjusted." —— Decrypt

### 关于 NFT + culture cross-over

> "NFTs are bringing new people into the space in a way that nothing else in cryptocurrency has. I've been waiting for my brother, for example, who's outside of crypto and isn't that interested in finance, to get excited about crypto. That's happened with something like NBA Top Shot." —— Unchained

> "NFTs are bringing in people who are interested in sports or music or art or culture outside of crypto in a way that nothing else has. Up until NFTs, it was very kind of finance, money..." —— Decrypt

> "It's not an easy experience. Part of that is what makes it fun, though. You have to have a certain level of curiosity in playing a game and figuring it out... There might not ever be this silver bullet [of UX improvement]." —— Decrypt

### 关于 Solana 2021 NFT（批评性）

> "Where I would be a bit more skeptical of things like Solana is that I haven't really seen anything new that's really pushing the space forward. If you look at Solana right now and NFTs on Solana, you have SolPunks or whatever, you have Sol Apes, all of these variants of things that are on Ethereum. And that's great, because it's bringing new people in, but these things can be fleeting... When you see so much noise around something in the short term, and you don't see any true innovation there, it can be a good time to be a bit skeptical." —— Decrypt 2021

### 关于 2018 Twitter 反省

> "One of the things I've learned is a lot of people in crypto tend to focus too much on public perception and press. In the last year, I've learned that stuff isn't that important. There's different strategies people can adopt, but in the last year a lot of people have tried to go all in on social media and Twitter. I've tweeted a lot in the past year but I've also tried to tone it down, and focus on being a good partner to the founders I've invested in." —— Fortune 2018

### 关于 Libra（反对）

> "I don't believe Libra will be meaningful long-term." —— Unconfirmed 2019

### 关于 BTC / ETH 5 年展望（2018）

> "I don't think Bitcoin will cross $10,000 again by the end of this year. But in the next five years, it's very likely there will be another boom much higher than the one last year and the price will be over $100,000."

> "I see a world in five or 10 years when Bitcoin is a widely used store of value and is the digital gold, and Ethereum is the most widely used crypto platform and where Ether is the digital oil." —— Fortune 2018（事后看：BTC 2021 破 $60K，2024 破 $100K，2025 刷新高）

### 关于 investment strategy（2018 四大 category）

> "We're focused on four categories of investment:
> 1) Stable coin companies like MakerDAO and Basis
> 2) Marketplaces in general, including those that help with staking such as Tezos and Vest
> 3) Scalable solutions, especially Bloxroute
> 4) Decentralized exchanges" —— Fortune 2018
