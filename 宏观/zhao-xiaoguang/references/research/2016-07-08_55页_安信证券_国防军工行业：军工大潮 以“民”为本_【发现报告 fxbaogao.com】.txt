军工大潮 以‚民‛为本 
■顶层设计加快扫清体制障碍，军民融合向实质性阶段推进。2016 年作
为军民融合十三五规划开局之年，在上升为国家战略之后，军民融合将成为未来
几年军工投资较为确定的方向，军民深度融合及资本证券化趋势已经明确。目前
我国军民融合度在30%左右， 2016 年3 月17 日国防科工局发布《2016 年国防科
工局军民融合专项计划》旨在加快推进国防科技工业军民融合深度发展。3 月12
日习近平主席强调要强化大局意识，站在党和国家事业发展全局高度思考问题、
推动工作做到责任到位、措施到位、落实到位；要着力解决制约军民融合发展的
体制性障碍、结构性矛盾、政策性问题，这预示着军民融合要扫除障碍加快推进。 
■军工信息化是‚十三五‛国防投入重要方向。5 月中央军委颁发《军队
建设发展‚十三五‛规划纲要》提出未来五年国防信息化中军事通信、电子对抗、
指挥控制、安全加密、导航定位等成为重点建设领域。信息化建设将构建能够打
赢信息化战争、有效履行使命任务的中国特色现代军事力量体系： 
• 高精尖武器装备升级换代需求对各分系统及单机设备提出更高指标要求，拉
动整个基础工业尤其电子产业技术替换提升。具体技术方向包括芯片、
FPGA，红外技术，先进导航制导与控制、量子通信、自主可控、新材料、
大数据、VR&AR 等技术。民参军后，整个航电系统均面临从上游到下游的
迭代替换。将激活整个军工电子千亿级市场空间。 
• 预计未来5 年指挥专网C4ISR 升级、舰载和机载军用雷达、量子通信加密、
水下防御作战系统成为通信行业中民参军增长最快的细分市场，军用C4ISR
系统通信网络建设加速，未来三年C4ISR 投资年复合增长率超过25%，预计
2018 年投资达1440 亿元，产业链上组网设备、终端、上游器件等将受益。 
• 国防信息安全细分领域建议重点发展网络与信息安全、卫星导航系统、电磁
仿真与信号处理、海洋信息化等领域。 
■ ‚一代材料，一代飞机‛军工材料奠定空天装备基石。高温合金是军
民航空发动机及地面燃气轮机热端部件不可替代的关键结构材料。在先进航空发
动机中，高温合金用量占发动机总重量高达60%以上。结合军机、民机、燃气轮
机、汽车涡轮增压以及核能需求，我们测算未来20 年我国高温合金年均母合金
销售规模662 亿元，年均叶片维修市场规模1003 亿元。 
■投资建议：十三五规划开局之年，顶层设计提升战略高度、强化大局观念，
落实责任到位及扫除制度障碍，军民融合为未来军工板块较为确定的投资方向： 
• 军民融合上升为国家战略，业绩弹性较大的建议重点关注华讯方舟、天海防
务、航新科技、天银机电、海兰信等； 
• 国防信息化重点推荐中航电测（力学测控和智能配电配套新机型、高过载
MEMS 放量），中航光电（受益整个军工电子增长），火炬电子（新材料），
紫光国芯（军工半导体），欧比特（宇航级芯片，卫星平台及卫星大数据），
振华科技（CEC 军工电子平台），久之洋（红外探测、激光测距新星）, 凯
乐科技（量子通信，自主可控，军民警融合发展）、三力士、盛路通信；关
注景嘉微（军用GPU），金信诺。 
• 软件推荐久远银海、同有科技、佳讯飞鸿、华力创通、启明星辰、卫士通； 
• 国防军工新材料板块推荐万泽股份、银邦股份、云南锗业；关注云海金属。 
■风险提示：国家政策推进缓慢，军民融合产业不达预期，周边局势紧张。

Big Military Wave, Based on ‚Civilian‛ 
■The top design speeds up to sweep away systematic barriers, promoting the military-civilian 
integration to step to substantial stage. At the beginning year of the 13th Five Plan in 2016, 
military-civilian integration has been lifted to the level of national strategy, which indicates that it will be the 
relatively definite direction for military investment in the near future years, and that the trend of deep 
military-civilian integration and capital securitization is undoubted. Our military-civilian integration rate is only 
about 30% now, therefore, the national defense science and industry bureau released “Military-civilian 
Integration Special Plan by National Defense Science and Industry Bureau in 2016”on 17th, March, aiming to 
promote the deep development of military-civilian integration. On March 12, Chairman Jinping Xi 
emphasized on the importance of thinking on the level of the whole party and national development 
with a holistic view, as well as implementing responsibility, proper measures and tasks when 
promoting work, and he also asked to solve the systematic barriers, structural contradictions, policy 
problems hindering military-civilian integration, which predicts the military-civilian integration will be 
accelerated by sweeping away the barriers.  
 
■Military informatization is the important direction of military defense investment of “The 13th Five 
Year Plan”. According to “The 13th Five Plan’s Outline on Army Construction and Development” issued by 
CMC on May, military communication, electronic warfare, command control, secure encryption and 
navigation and location in defense informatization will become the key fields in the future five years. The 
informatization construction will build modern military system with Chinese characteristics which will win 
informatization war and perform tasks effectively. 
• 
The demand for upgrading and updating of high-grade, high-precision and advanced weapon 
equipment makes higher index requirements for the subsystems and single unit equipments, driving 
the whole infrastructure industry especially the electronic industry to be updated. The concrete 
technology is consisted of chips, FPGA, infrared technology, advanced navigation guidance and 
control,  quantum communication, self-control, new material, big data, VR&AR and so on. After 
military-civilian integration, the whole  avionics system will confront the upgrading and updating 
from upstream to downstream, which will activate the whole military electronic market of hundred 
billion level.  
• 
It is predicted the updating special command network C4ISR, ship-borne and air-borne military radar, 
quantum communication encryption and underwater defense combat system will become the 
submarkets at the fastest growth as civilian-attending-military in communication industry in the near 5 
years. The construction of communication network of military C41SR system is accelerated. It is 
estimated C4ISR’s compounded annual growth rate in the near 3 years will exceed 25% and the 
investment on it will be up to 144 billion in 2018, which will benefit networking equipment, terminal 
equipment and upstream devices in the industry chain.  
• 
In the defense information safety sub-fields, it is suggested that network and information safety, 
satellite navigation system, electromagnetic simulation and signal processing, ocean informatization 
should be emphasized.   
■ “New generation of airplane depends on new generation of material”. Military material is the base 
of space equipment. High temperature alloy is the irreplaceable critical structural material for engines of 
military civilian aviation and hot end parts of ground gas turbine. The weight of high temperature alloy 
accounts for over 60% of the total engine for the advanced aviation engines. We expect that annual 
average sales scale of mother alloy of high temperature alloy will be 66.2 billion and annual average scale 
of blade repairing market will be 100.3 billion in the near 20 years.  
■ Investment advice: Military-civilian integration is the relative definite investment direction for the future 
military sector, for in the beginning year of the 13th Five Plan, the top design aims at promoting it to the 
strategic level, emphasizing on holding holistic view, implementing responsibilities and eliminating system 
barriers.  
• 
For defense informatization, we highly recommend Avic Zemic(new product model equipped with 
mechanical measurement and control and intelligent power distribution, high overload MEMS), Jonhon 
Photonics (benefited from the growth of whole military electronics),      Torch Electronic(new material 
applied), Uni IC(military semi-conductor), Obiter(space-level chips, space platform and big data), Zhenhua 
Technology (CEC military electronic platform),      Jiuzhiyang Infrared( new star in 
infrared detection,  laser ranging field), Kaile Technology (quantum communication, self-control, military- 
civilian- police integration), Sanlux, Shenglu Communication . Focus on Jingjia Micro (military GPU), 
Kingsignal. 
• 
For the software, we recommend Jiuyuan Yinhai, Tongyou Technology, Jiaxun, Hwa Creat, Venustec, 
and Westone. 
• 
For defense military new material sector, we recommend Wedge, Yinbang, Yunnan Germanium. 
Focus on Yunhai Metal. 
■Risk warning: Slow promotion of national policy, less-than-expected military-civilian integration, and tense 
border situation.
